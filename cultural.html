Cultural Events

A HIGH ENERGY,DYNAMIC DISCO & DANCE EVENTS

Boogie Lover Band is a highly charged party of professional musicians solely committed to getting you onto the dance floor.

Well qualified and successful in their individual endeavors, they come together in a mix of vivid colors and electrifying sounds to pump out authentic disco hits of the 70’s and 80’s right through to the latest dance hits of the new millennium.

With a lineup that includes percussion, horns, strings and electronics in addition to a super tight rhythm section and almighty vocals, you’d be assured of a BIG and FULL sound to knock you off your feet.  All tunes are creatively and thoroughly arranged and performed to perfection.  We do not ‘wing’ our tunes.

In addition to corporate functions and large community events, we also cater to weddings, private parties and pub/café gigs.  No event is too large or too small for us!  With our musicians having firm roots in jazz, we are also able to provide soothing background music for dinning before we bring the roof down with our dance numbers!

Nothing beats having a live band at your next grand event!  Nothing beats having Boogie Lover Band orchestrate it for you!  Be dazzled by our brilliant visuals!  Be awed by our rich and extravagant sound!  Be elevated by our spirit to boogie and party!

Peter Robinson

Bass

Peter is an experienced music teacher and session musician who has been living in Perth for 10 years to pursue his passion for music. Formerly from the South-West he graduated at the Western Australian Academy of Performing Arts graduating in 2016 with a Bachelor in Music, Majoring in Contemporary Music. Peter continues to perform around Perth in original and corporate acts, recording in studios and touring around Western Australia for WAM festivals and the Perth music scene. Peter looks forward to deliver a disco inferno night bursting with dance moves and grooves from the era!

Maeve Lander

Trumpet

Maeve Lander

Trumpet

Maeve is an experienced musican and demon trumpeter, performing regularly in a wide variety of ensembles.  Originally trained in the classical music tradition at Perth Modern School, Maeve spent some years in the Australian Army Band, before diving into jazz and contemporary music performance.

A talented and versatile young musican, Maeve composes and arranges (any style, as long as it has a trumpet in it!) and is the creator / lead developer of Orpheus – a popular sheet music reader app for Android.

Alex Barker

Drums

Alex Barker

Drums

Alex started playing in bands in NSW during early high school. He has since toured up and down the east coast of Australia, performing on numerous sessions, and has appeared on the music video program RAGE. In 2012 Alex moved to Perth to study drums at the prestigious Western Australian Academy of Performing Arts (WAAPA), graduating in 2015.

Alex’s musical journey has seen him delve into the contrasting worlds of Hip Hop, Punk, Folk and Electronica, and everything in between. He has a keen ear for all aspects of musical performance and composition, having spent considerable time writing electronic music, as well as playing guitar, bass and his main instrument; the drums.

Malcolm Mah

Keys & Musical Director

Malcolm Mah

Keys & Musical Director

Malcolm Mah is testimony that one is never too old, or young, to switch careers and pick up an instrument in midlife. A graduate of the Western Australian Academy Of Performing Arts (WAAPA) in 2013, he finds himself busy as a bassist with numerous local bands and ensembles. Aside from laying down a solid groove on both the Electric and Double Bass, Malcolm is also actively involved arranging and production work for other artistes. He performs regularly with diverse acts including the Catz Meow (Tania Walker) in which he is also the arranger and musical director, Manhatton Hot Swing(Phil Hatton), Beans Bumas Boys Of Rhythm, Rhythm 22, The Miracle Band, The Merindas, Lions Big Band, Kenny Austin, Alkatraz, to name a few.

Malcolm’s love for disco stemmed from his countless visits to the discotheques during his formative years. His mission is to keep disco and dance music alive with an amazing outfit of like minded performers.
