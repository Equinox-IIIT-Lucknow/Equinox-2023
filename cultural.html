EMBRACING THE NOSTALGIA

EQUINOX 2022

With the theme of Equinox'22 being "Embracing the Nostalgia", we took everyone onboard with us to the times as good as ever, with a new version of themselves, the one that triumphed over all the hardships. Equinox was a 3-day extravaganza filled with adventurous and educational events that drove us to think outside the box and left everyone with a hue of reminiscent memories.

Celebrating the coder, the reader, the learner, and the leader, Equinox provided everyone a platform to showcase their ideas and build upon them, to morph their unrelenting imagination into a reality. We had fun exciting contests for all students, experts, and beginners to code, learn and experience the wonders of technology.

A HIGH ENERGY,DYNAMIC DISCO & DANCE EVENTS EQUINOX 2023

Nukkad Naatak

"Nukkad Naatak" differs from traditional drama in that it is never performed on a stage and is always used to draw attention to a social problem. In Equinox '22, the dramatics club Goonj gathered its team to perform a beautiful "Nukkad Naatak" of its own to draw attention to the issue of the third gender, the transgender people.

Mime Act

Goonj's debut mime performance focused on the unseen suffering of the transgender population, and were successful in educating the audience on the topic. We should respect one another's differences and embrace one another for who we are.

The Musicale

Estrella, the music club, illuminated Equinox '22 with harmonizing performances that transported everyone to a nostalgic realm of songs, where the musician in all of us resides, together with unending enthusiasm and zest and each of our passions for music.

The Zestful Dance Staging

When one dances, one has that same transcendental emotion whether they are in front of a show with carefully picked backgrounds or in front of an auditorium full of familiar faces. Zephyr, the dance club of IIITL, celebrated Equinox'22 with its ferocious dance displays and loving reminiscence.
